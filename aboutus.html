Why?

Footpath School?

Footpath School is an initiative run by Mohit Educational and Social Institution. This organization is registered through the Charity Commissioner. Our organization is working through many schools to bring poor and budding students into the stream of education. Our organization works for children who are deprived of education Our organization is working to bring children who are not interested in education into the mainstream of education. We work to bring such children into the mainstream of education by giving them a basic education.

Mohit Sahare

purpose

Our Aim

The organization works to provide free education and health information to boys and girls between the ages of one and sixteen, as well as free legal advice to prevent injustice to children.Also to create awareness about menstruation of teenage girls.Through our organization, we provide industry guidance to the children who want to start a business as well as the capital required for the business.

Story

Start of Journey

The founding president of this organization is Mohit Satish Sahare, who has come out of a very difficult situation. He has a newspaper agency at his house and distributes newspapers every morning.Many such children are deprived of education to meet while distributing morning newspapers.And then in the center of Wardha city, one such Versus Please started educating children who are deprived of education. Initially it was very difficult to go to the area as it was forbidden for outsiders to enter the area but after explaining to many people they allowed them to enter the area and then started teaching the children.The initiative came to be known as Footpath School as all these children were educated on the sidewalk. Initially this initiative was started by Mohit Sahare and since then many people have joined this initiative
