Blood Donation

Tue Jul 28 2020
Jawaharlal Nehru Medical college,D.M.I.M.S (sawangi) Wardha

  • Purpose

    Through this initiative, our poor and needy people who are in dire need of blood connect with us. Our group members or we challenge people to make blood available to patients who do not receive blood.

  • Format

    1)Seeing patient information. 2)Providing blood donors for patients after that. 3)Paying the blood donor for travel expenses through the organization. 4)Providing free blood to the needy.

  • Outcome

    1)Free blood was provided to patients who needed it. 2)Many patients come from outside the village and we help them when no one is around. 3) The patient's relatives save some money.
